Food Order App

A web app where users can order food.

Check out the app here!

Tools: javascript, React - components and hook

Display

Users are given a menu where they can add what they want to eat to the cart.

The cart is found at the top right corner where they can find their overall order.

Menu

On the menu, you can input how many items you want to add to cart.

Cart

Users can view their order and the total cost in the cart. They can adjust their order as well by adding or removing items.

Whenever they are ready, they can order.

Empty

If the cart is empty, users will not have the option to order.

Inspiration

Currently, local stores and restaurants are all closed. To limit contact while still being able to order food, I created an app that would help users order food to learn react.
